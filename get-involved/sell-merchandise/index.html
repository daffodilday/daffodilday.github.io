Daffodil Day Merchandise

One of the best ways you can help Cancer Council reach our fundraising goal is to order Daffodil Day merchandise. Every item you sell makes a difference to cancer patients and their families all over Australia.

Merchandise can be ordered by individuals, clubs, schools or workplaces, which will be delivered for free (excluding pins). Some items can be ordered at no cost to you, or you can pay up front. It’s up to you!

Simply sell what you can during the month of August and return any unsold merchandise, with the proceeds you have raised, to your state or territory Cancer Council. Ready to order some merchandise? Simply choose your state or territory below:

 

Order merchandise >
 

Or call our events hotline on 1300 65 65 85.

 

Order Fresh Daffodils

If you are from ACT, NT, QLD, SA or WA, you can order fresh daffodils via our events hotline on 1300 65 65 85.

By selling merchandise, you are helping Cancer Council fund vital cancer research, provide early detection and prevention programs and coordinate a network of cancer support groups, services and programs.
