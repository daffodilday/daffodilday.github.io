Daffodil Day 2015 - Early Bird Banking Competition

 1. Instructions to enter form part of these conditions.

2. To be eligible to enter the Daffodil Day 2015 Early Banking Competition, registered supporters must bank a minimum of $228 by Friday 11 September 2015. Entry is automatic based on banking to your state or territory Cancer Council organisation by Friday 11 September 2015.

3. The promoter of this competition is Cancer Council Australia. Address: Level 14, 477 Pitt Street, Sydney NSW 2000. ABN 91 130 793 725.

4. Employees of Cancer Council Australia and its members are ineligible to enter.

5. Each valid entrant will only be eligible for one automatic entry to win.

6. Competition is open to Australian residents only

7. The start date of the competition is Wednesday 10 June 2015.

8. Last entry closes 11.59pm Friday 11 September 2015.

9. The prize will be drawn at Cancer Council Australia’s office at 12pm on Monday 21 September 2015.

10. The winners will be notified in writing.

11. Prizes will be awarded to the person named in the entry. However, in a dispute, will be awarded to the email account holder of the entry mechanism used to submit their entry.

12. The prize is not transferrable and is not redeemable for cash.

13. The prize will be handed out within twenty one (21) days of the draw.

15. Permit numbers:  Authorised under permit numbers: ACT TP 15/05980

16. The Winner will be announced in The Australian on Friday 2 October 2015 and will be uploaded to the Daffodil Day website on Friday 2 October 2015: www.daffodilday.com.au/win

17. The prize is a PARKROYAL Darling Harbour Getaway for two people including:

  • Return economy flights from any Australian state capital to Sydney for two people
  • Two Nights’ accommodation, for two people, including buffet breakfast at the PARKROYAL Darling Harbour
  • Transfers to and from Sydney airport
  • Travel insurance for two people

 (Total estimated prize value AUD $1500 and is valid to 31 September 2016).

18. An unclaimed prize draw will be held at 12pm on Wednesday 23 December 2015 to re-draw the prize of:

  • Return economy flights from any Australian state capital to Sydney for two people.
  • Two Nights’ accommodation, for two people, including buffet breakfast at PARKROYAL Darling Harbour.
  • Transfers to and from Sydney airport
  • Travel insurance for two people

Address of re-draw: Cancer Council Australia Level 14, 477 Pitt Street, Sydney NSW 2000. The winner of the unclaimed prize draw will be notified in writing.  

19. Once the Prize vouchers have been distributed to the winner, it is the winners responsibility to liaise directly to the Reservations Department at PARKROYAL Darling Harbour to organise dates to redeem the prize. Contact details for the Reservations Department 02 9261 1188

20. The winner MUST inform Cancer Council Australia of dates booked, and Cancer Council Australia will liaise with the winner, to coordinate return flights for the winner and their guest.

21. Extending or adding passengers, dates and destinations is not permitted.

22. No frequent flyer points are awarded and no upgrades are permitted

23. Items of a personal nature including but not limited to drinks, spending money, snacks, most meals, additional sightseeing, and entertainment are not included.

24. Should a flight be cancelled or become available, we will endeavour to place the passengers on an alternate carrier.

25. Cancer Council Australia will not be held responsible for additional incidental costs incurred by the winner and their guest, as a part of the prize.
