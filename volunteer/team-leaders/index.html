Lead the way

Volunteer for Daffodil Day >

Team leaders are important for ensuring Daffodil Day runs smoothly and successfully.

As a team leader, you are responsible for managing a Daffodil Day site and a team of volunteers. This includes being the primary contact for volunteers and ensuring their safety.

You will be the point-of-contact for Cancer Council, liaising with us to coordinate your site. We will supply you with the information and resources you need, such as tablecloths, daffodils and merchandise to sell.

You will also complete any paperwork, including making sure volunteers sign in at the start of their shift and sign out when they have finished. You will supply volunteers with an ID sticker to wear while they volunteer.

Your responsibility includes looking after the money banking the funds at the end of the day.

If you think you have what it takes to lead a team, register to volunteer and we will be in touch. If you have already registered to volunteer and would like to be a team leader, please contact our Events Hotline on 1300 65 65 85.
