Volunteer this Daffodil Day, 28th August

Volunteer for Daffodil Day >

 

You can register to volunteer for Daffodil Day 2015 now, simply click the button above to find your location.

Volunteering, for just a few hours, involves working with a team of volunteers selling merchandise and fresh daffodils to the general public on Daffodil Day. Your help means Cancer Council can continue to raise much-needed funds that go toward:

  • Funding programs to prevent the risk of cancer
  • Raising cancer awareness in the community
  • Funding ground breaking research
  • Running Cancer Council 13 11 20. A free, confidential telephone information and support service

 

If you would like more information on volunteering, please call our Events Hotline on 1300 65 65 85.
