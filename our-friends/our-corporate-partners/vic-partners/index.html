Mandala Art is dedicated to raising awareness of all types of illnesses affecting Australians through their Healthy Living Through Art initiatives. Through their community events and mosaic art classes, they provide an outlet for people to be creative and enjoy the therapeutic benefits of a positive life outlook. In keeping with their vision for Healthy Living Through Art, Mandala Art is once again supporting the Cancer Council during this year’s Daffodil Day campaign. Following last year’s Garden of Hope at Federation Square, Mandala Art will be creating an installation of art pieces to drive cancer awareness and promote positive lifestyles for the mind and body.

www.mandalaart.com.au

My Chemist Pharmacies provide Australians with the Best in Health at the Best Price, whilst always providing advice and updated information. My Chemist has been a valued supporter of Cancer Council's Daffodil Day for over 7 years and will continue to support Daffodil Day in 2014, along with their partner bodies Chemist Warehouse and Beauty Spot.

www.mychemist.com.au

Chemist Warehouse Pharmacies provide Australians with the Best in Health at the Best Price, whilst always providing advice and updated information. Chemist  Warehouse has been a valued supporter of Cancer Council’s Daffodil Day for over 7 years and will continue to support Daffodil Day in 2014, along with their partner bodies My Chemist and Beauty Spot.

www.mychemist.com.au

IGA supermarkets, with over 300 stores in Victoria are once again proud to support Cancer Council's Daffodil Day. In the heart of every IGA storeowner is a firm belief that every bit helps. That's why IGA stores across Victoria will support Daffodil Day by displaying merchandise boxes in their stores.

www.iga.net.au

Fastway Couriers are proudly supporting Cancer Council’s Daffodil Day this year by delivering fresh daffodils across Melbourne free-of-charge. Their support enables us to spread the message of hope to people far and wide, and enable more funds to go directly to the fight against cancer.

www.fastway.com.au

Total Office National was founded in Victoria in 1991 and is proudly 100% Australian owned and operated. As a group we now have over 180 offices nationally and service a vast Australian market for all their office requirements ranging from stationery, furniture, custom printing right through to work wear, kitchen and janitorial products.

Total Office National has been a proud and long-standing supporter of the Cancer Council through various promotional and fundraising activities and most recently through our exclusive customer reward program. We are now delighted to actively participate in the Cancer Council's Daffodil Day through the sale of fundraising merchandise to our customer base.

www.totaloffice.com.au
