Get the most out of your workplace fundraising

There are many good reasons for your workplace to get involved in Daffodil Day. It’s an opportunity for you and your colleagues to raise money for a great cause. Daffodil Day is Cancer Council’s biggest fundraising event, helping fund cancer research, support and services. 

Daffodil Day can also be a team building exercise. Get the whole workplace involved. Make it fun by dressing in yellow, decorating the space in yellow and hosting a morning or afternoon tea. Use your creativity to think of ways to raise extra funds, such as asking for a coin donation to join in the festivities. We hold a fancy hat competition at Cancer Council’s Sydney office, with our CEO choosing the winner.

Of course, we will also supply you with daffodils and Daffodil Day merchandise for staff and customers or clients to purchase. Merchandise ranges in price, so there’s something suitable for every budget. And you will receive a resource pack, with everything you need to run a successful Daffodil Day, including posters to place around the workplace. 

Here are some quick hints and ideas for raising money at your workplace:

  • Wear yellow. Encourage everyone to wear a daffodil pin or something yellow on Daffodil Day. Prior to the day, send out regular reminders. Organise a donation box in a prominent position of the office, and make several trips around the office to collect donations so you don’t miss people who aren’t at their desks.
  • Team up with an existing event. Organise your fundraising activities to take advantage of an event that is already happening, such as your work or sport team meeting, an office celebration or morning tea.
  • Decorate the office in yellow. Use the posters and balloons to decorate your office or add a personal touch with some streamers or party hats. You can download posters to print from our Downloads and Resources section. 
  • Compete to raise funds. Hold competitions with your friends or work colleagues to see who can raise the most money.
  • Dollar-for-dollar matching.  Ask your employer to support your fundraising efforts by matching the funds you raise. You can download a dollar matching template from our Downloads and Resources seciton.
  • Hold a yellow event. Why not organise your very own healthy Daffodil Day breakfast, invite a guest speaker, auction, raffle or competition?
